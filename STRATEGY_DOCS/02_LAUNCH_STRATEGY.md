# ScoutVerse: Launch and Marketing Strategy

## The 90/10 Rule for Completion
My honest suggestion is to avoid seeking perfection for the entire app. Instead, focus on the "Perfect Hero Feature." As long as the ScoutVision AI report looks world-class and the video upload is smooth, investors and users will forgive a simple dashboard. The goal is a "Wow" factor on the primary output.

## Stealth Marketing Strategy
You do not need a finished app to start marketing. You should take screen recordings of the AI analyzing famous players or local stars and post these "sneak peeks" on LinkedIn or Twitter to build a waitlist. This creates a group of users ready to click "Sign Up" on Day 1, which investors love to see.

## Social Proof and Authority
Marketing should not just be about the app, but about the talent it finds. Position ScoutVerse as the authority. Use the app to find an underrated player and post their report. If that player gains attention, ScoutVerse becomes the "Oracle" of football.

## The Beta Tester Bridge
Before the public launch, give login access to one real agent or coach. Let them find the confusing parts. Fixing usability issues based on real feedback makes the app look significantly more professional during an investor demo.

## Recommended Action Plan
The immediate path is to finish the core loop of Upload to Analysis to Professional Report. Once that is rock-solid, start the marketing while polishing the smaller secondary features.
