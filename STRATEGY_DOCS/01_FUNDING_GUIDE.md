# ScoutVerse: Funding and Investor Interest Strategy

## Market Analysis and Investor Appeal
The SportsTech and AI-Scouting market is currently exploding. Investors are drawn to projects like ScoutVerse for three primary reasons. First is Scalability; traditional scouting is expensive in terms of travel and salaries, whereas a digital platform can scout thousands of players instantly. Second is the Unseen Talent Gap; there is a massive global market in regions like Africa, South America, and Eastern Europe where elite talent is often never seen. Third is Objectivity; clubs are moving away from biased agent reports and toward objective data.

## Strategic Funding Roadmap

**Phase 1: Proof of Concept (Current Stage)**
The focus is on finishing the MVP. We need a gallery of 10 "Gold Standard" reports generated by the AI that look professional and data-heavy to serve as our primary asset.

**Phase 2: The Traction Phase (Next 3 Months)**
Before seeking big venture capital, we aim to get 5 to 10 real scouts or academy coaches to use the platform for free. The goal is to collect testimonials that prove our AI helps them find talent in minutes instead of hours.

## Primary Funding Sources
One option is SportsTech Accelerators like Hype Sports Innovation or ADvantage Sports Tech Fund which specifically look for AI tools in sports. Another is Angel Investors, specifically ex-players or sports executives on LinkedIn who invest in passion projects with high tech potential. A third is B2B Pre-Sales, where local academies pay a monthly fee to have their players analyzed.

## The Investor X-Factor
The core pitch to investors is that we are building the Bloomberg Terminal for Football Talent. We combine raw physics with LLM-based scouting logic to give every player a digital, objective profile.

## Professional Valuation Assessment
For a Pre-Seed round at the MVP stage, a realistic investment target is $150,000 to $250,000 for 10% to 15% equity. This capital would be used to scale the technical infrastructure and handle the first 5,000 users.
